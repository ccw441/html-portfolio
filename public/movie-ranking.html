The Best Movies According to Chelsea

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Lion King

This is my favorite Disney film. It's a sweet and heartwarming film that can be watched over and over again, and still love every moment of the movie.

The Lady and the Tramp

This is my second favorite Disney film. It's a great story of opposites, uniting. It's another sweet, heartwarming film that has dogs as the main characters.

The Prince of Egypt

This is my third favorite animated film. I believe it's a Dreamworks production, and it's a great story of redemption, inspiration and revolution. It's a wonderful film that mirrors the epics and majestic adventures expressed in human culture.
